# Venture Tycoon

Let's discover what is **Venture Tycoon**.


### Basic information

- Venture Tycoon is a business tycoon game about buying businesses, managing them, and growing them. Principally, you will be buying businesses, upgrading them, and using them to generate income. You have to control supplies, demand, employees, and prices to maximize your profits.
- You start with a small amount of money and a small business. You can upgrade your business to make more money. You can also buy other businesses to make more money. You can also invest in stocks to make money.
- Game generates income in 15 minutes intervals, but it's not supposed to be idle game.

## What to expect?

- Choose building, each one with specific type, disctrict, buffs, or even perks that help you to become emperor of the city. More info about buildings you can find **[here](basics/building-business-multipliers.md)**.
- Each building can host only one business which is affected by the disctrics, buffs, or building type.
- Your goal is to build empire, conquer the city, and become the richest person in the world.
